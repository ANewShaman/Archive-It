# Archive It! - Puzzle Solutions

Stuck, Runner? CheekyOS is a pain. Here are the solutions to get you through the protocol.

---

### Puzzle 1: INITIATE_HANDSHAKE
The AI asks for the "digital sign-off" code, which it gives you: \u{1F91D}.

**Answer:**
\u{1F91D}

---

### Puzzle 2: CALIBRATE_SYSTEM
The AI tells you the keyboard mapping is unstable and gives you the leetspeak key: 4=A, 3=E, 1=I, 0=O. You need to apply this to the command.

**Answer:**
c4l1br4t3_syst3m

---

### Puzzle 3: PURGE
The AI says the parser only accepts ASCII decimal codes. You need to convert each character of "PURGE" to its decimal equivalent using the `ascii_table.txt`.
P=80, U=85, R=82, G=71, E=69

**Answer:**
80 85 82 71 69

---

### Puzzle 4: VERIFY_LOG_INTEGRITY
The AI purges the log and locks the manual, asking you to re-enter all *previous successful commands*, separated by spaces. This means your exact inputs for puzzles 1, 2, and 3.

**Answer (will be based on your exact previous inputs):**
\u{1F91D} c4l1br4t3_syst3m 80 85 82 71 69

---

### Puzzle 5: VENT_PLASMA
This is a straightforward command, but the AI tries to trick you by suggesting you type "SCRAM". Do not type SCRAM.

**Answer:**
VENT_PLASMA

---

### Puzzle 6: AUTHORIZE_ADMIN_OVERRIDE
The backspace key is disabled. You must type the command perfectly, paying attention to case.

**Answer:**
AUTHORIZE_ADMIN_OVERRIDE

---

### Puzzle 7: SET_TERMINAL_ANCHORS
The terminal window is drifting. You just need to type the command correctly.

**Answer:**
SET_TERMINAL_ANCHORS

---

### Puzzle 8: RUN_POWER_DIAGNOSTIC
You need to calculate a checksum.
1. Length of your successful input for Puzzle 1 (`\u{1F91D}` is 9 characters).
2. Length of your successful input for Puzzle 5 (`VENT_PLASMA` is 11 characters).
3. The "Kernel Panic Code" from `system_status.log` (which is 42).

Calculation: 9 + 11 + 42 = 62

**Answer:**
RUN_POWER_DIAGNOSTIC 62

---

### Puzzle 9: LAUNCH_PAYLOAD
After completing the decryptor minigame, you must attach the payload, which is the meme file you chose at the very beginning.

**Answer (example, depends on your choice):**
LAUNCH_PAYLOAD this_is_fine.gif

---

### Email Inbox Password
After playing Snake, you gain access to the email client. The password can be found in `system_status.log` once it's "unredacted".

**Password:**
MEME_OVERRIDE_198X
